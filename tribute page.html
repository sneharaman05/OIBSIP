Ranbir kapoor

"Happiness exists only if you have a lot of people to share it with."

Ranbir Kapoor is an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi-language films.

A Stellar Career

He is one of the highest-paid actors of Hindi cinema and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012..

About Kapoor's Family

Ranbir Kapoor is not just a talented actor, but also a charismatic personality who has won hearts worldwide. Here's what makes him special:
